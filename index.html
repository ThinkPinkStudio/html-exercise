About the ThinkPink project

The ThinkPink project is an initiative aimed at fostering a community of passionate developers around the world more especially Africa. Our mission is to create innovative solutions and build a strong network of individuals who are dedicated to making a positive impact in the world of software development.

Through collaboration, creativity, and continuous learning, we strive to empower our members to achieve their goals and contribute to the growth of the tech industry in Africa and around the world.
